About The ARTAS

Twelve Years of Celebrating Unscripted TV

The American Reality Television Awards was founded in 2013 with a singular mission: to celebrate the shows, hosts, and crews that make unscripted television one of the most powerful forces in entertainment. What began as a passionate fan-driven initiative has grown into the premier awards show for reality television.

Under the executive production of Kristen Moss & Andrew Ward, the ARTAS has recognized hundreds of shows across 12 seasons, building a global community of fans who believe reality TV deserves its moment in the spotlight.
