The Hall of Records

Notable Streaks & Dynasties

These shows defined a decade of unscripted television dominance at the ARTAS.

Deadliest Catch
✦ Dynasty Record

The most decorated crew in ARTAS history. Deadliest Catch took home the "Badass Crew" award for Outstanding Production Crew in four consecutive years — a streak unmatched by any other show in the award's history.

2013 2014 2015 2016 2018 2020
Pitbulls & Parolees
✦ Feel Good Royalty

Tia Torres' show captured the hearts of ARTAS voters year after year. Pitbulls & Parolees won Feel Good Show multiple times across the first six annual awards, alongside Overall Show and Docu-Series honors.

2014 (Docu & Feel Good) 2015 (Feel Good) 2016 (Feel Good) 2018 (Feel Good) 2019 (Overall)
RuPaul's Drag Race
✦ Multi-Category Legend

RuPaul's Drag Race swept across categories from Creative Challenge and Judging Panel to Overall Show and Outstanding Host — with RuPaul himself winning Best Host and the show taking Overall Show at the 10th Annual.

2014 (Creative Challenge) 2016 (Creative Challenge, Host) 2018 (Judging Panel, Shocking Moment) 2023 (Overall, Host, Judging)
