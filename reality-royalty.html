The ARTAS equivalent of “Best Actor” — honoring the most impactful stars in unscripted television since 2013.

The Award

What Is the Royalty Award?

Whereas scripted shows award “Best Actor,” the Reality Royalty Award recognizes someone who had a great impact on unscripted television. Since 2013, The ARTAS have let the public decide who reigns supreme each year — the Queen, the King, or both. The honoree is chosen entirely by public vote, making this the most democratic prestige award in television.
