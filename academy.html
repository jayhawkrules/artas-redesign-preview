About the Academy

What Is the Academy?

Your Home in Reality Television

The Academy of Reality Television is a free membership program created by The ARTAS for the professionals and talent who make unscripted television happen every day.

Whether you work at a network, a production company, or as a freelancer — or whether you appear on screen — the Academy is your professional community. Members receive invitations to exclusive networking events, access to the ARTAS industry ballot, and a home within the broader reality TV world.

The Academy holds regular networking events that connect members across departments and disciplines. It’s the community the reality TV industry has needed.

🏭
Network & Production Staff Executives, producers, coordinators, and crew at networks and production companies
📷
Freelancers Independent professionals who work across the unscripted TV landscape
👤
Agents & Managers Talent representatives who work with reality TV personalities and talent
🌟
On-Screen Talent Cast members and personalities who have appeared on unscripted series
Eligibility

Who Can Join?

The Academy is open to current 2024–2025 Reality TV industry professionals who have worked at least one role in the unscripted television space.

Current Reality TV Industry Professionals

You qualify if you have held at least one professional role in the reality television industry during the 2024–2025 season. This includes a broad range of roles across all areas of production and talent.

Network executives and development staff
Showrunners and executive producers
Producers and story producers
Directors and field producers
Post-production and editors
Casting directors and associates
Talent agents and managers
On-screen cast and personalities
Production coordinators and APs
Freelance crew and contractors
Digital and social media teams
PR and publicity professionals
ⓘ

Minimum Requirement: At least one professional role in reality/unscripted television during the 2024–2025 season. All applications are reviewed by The ARTAS team to verify industry affiliation.
