Why Enter

What The ARTAS Means

Recognition from the longest-running awards show dedicated entirely to unscripted television.

🌎
National Recognition

Your series is spotlighted on a national stage alongside the most celebrated names in unscripted entertainment. ARTAS recognition carries genuine industry prestige built over 12 years.

👑
Fan-Voted Awards

Millions of public votes determine the winners across 26+ categories — meaning your nomination directly engages and energizes your audience. Real fans, real votes, real impact.

★
Elite Industry Roster

Join past honorees including Ellen DeGeneres, Gordon Ramsay, RuPaul, Steve Harvey, and Leah Remini. ARTAS alumni represent the very best unscripted television has produced.

📣
Promotional Opportunities

Nominees receive social media spotlights, press coverage, and promotional support across the ARTAS platform. Being nominated is a powerful marketing asset for your show and network.

Eligibility

Who Can Submit

The ARTAS welcomes all unscripted programming across every platform and genre.

  • Network, cable, and streaming unscripted series
  • Competition, docu-series, game show, and talent formats
  • Food, travel, lifestyle, and home programming
  • Digital-first and social media reality formats
  • International reality series airing in the US market
  • Series airing between July 2025 and June 2026
